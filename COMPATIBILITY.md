## How to Find Your TV Info

1. **Model number:** Settings → Support → About This TV
2. **Tizen version:** Settings → Support → Software Update (or About This TV)
3. **Year:** Usually encoded in the model number (e.g., "UN55**TU**7000" → TU = 2020)
